What Is Simplified Investing?


Simplified Investing is an online tool that you can use to analyze a company’s financial standing by computing and understanding financial ratios. Furthermore, these ratios are of great importance when analyzing a company as it indicates whether a company is financially stable and how well they perform. Simplified Investing is an ongoing project and this version focuses on a hands on market analysis; as the user will have input data and determine if the commuted ratios are sufficient for the company in the industry. Moreover, we are working hard to develop a recommendation system that will not only compute ratios, but also compare them to the industry standards to determine whether the company is worth investing, and if the stock is overvalued or undervalued.

Intro to Financial Analysis

For the users with little to no experience with finance or financial statements, understand what financial statements are, and how to leverage them to understand and gain insight into the financial performance of the company. Furthermore, learn the basis of financial statements such as the income statement and the balance sheet.


How to Get Started

Every publicly traded company is required to publish their financial statements every year. Learn where to find these financial statements of any publicly traded company. Not only that, learn how these statements could be useful to asses a company's financial standing.




Perform Financial Analysis

If you already know and understand financial statements and the basics of financial analysis you’re all set to compute ratios and better understand a company’s financial position for future investments.
