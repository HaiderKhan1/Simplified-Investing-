Financial Ratios

Market Prospect Ratios


Compare publicly traded companies stock prices with other financial measures like earnings and dividend rates to figure out a stock’s current and future market value.

Liquidity Ratios


Analyze the company's ability to pay of both current and long term liabilities as they come due

Profitability Ratios


Compare a company’s income statement accounts and analyze its ability to generate profits from it’s operation

Solvency Ratios


Measure a company’s ability to sustain operations by comparing debt levels to equity, assets and earnings.

Efficiency Ratios


Measure how well a company utilizes their assets to generate income

Financial Leverage Ratios


Measure the value of equity in a company by analyzing its overall debt picture

Coverage Ratios


Measure a company’s ability to pay its liabilities
